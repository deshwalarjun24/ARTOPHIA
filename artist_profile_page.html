Jane Doe

Jane Doe is a multidisciplinary artist whose work explores themes of memory, identity, and the natural world. Born in Seattle in 1985, she received her MFA from Rhode Island School of Design in 2010. Her practice spans painting, sculpture, and installation, often incorporating found objects and organic materials.

"My work investigates the intersection between human experience and the natural environment. Through a process-driven approach, I create pieces that reflect the fragility and resilience of both ecological systems and personal memory. I'm interested in how materials can embody time, transformation, and the ephemeral nature of existence."
